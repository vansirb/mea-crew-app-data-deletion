MEA Crew App - Data Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your personal data collected by the MEA Crew App, please send an email to our support team.

Email your request to: mazenmd19@gmail.com

In your email, please include:

  • Your app identifier (e.g., Android Advertising ID - you can usually find this in your device settings under Google > Ads > Your advertising ID, or simply state 'all data associated with the MEA Crew App on this device').
  • A clear statement that you wish to have your data deleted.

For data collected by third-party advertising services like Google AdMob, please note that you can manage your preferences for personalized ads and reset your advertising ID through your device's settings (e.g., in Android, go to Google > Ads). Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy provides further information on how they handle data.

We will review your request and take appropriate action within a reasonable timeframe, as required by applicable laws.
